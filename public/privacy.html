Privacy Policy for Voca Web

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Voca Web ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and web service.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Password: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (never in plain text)

Vocabulary Data

  • Words you save: The vocabulary words you choose to add to your personal collection
  • Study progress: Your learning statistics and progress data
  • Groups and categories: Custom organization you create for your words

Usage Information

  • Selected text: Words you highlight and look up (only processed when you actively use the extension)
  • Study activity: Time spent studying and practice session data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide dictionary lookup services
  • Save and sync your personal vocabulary across devices
  • Track your learning progress and provide statistics
  • Enable offline access to your saved words
  • Improve the service and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your vocabulary data is primarily stored locally on your device using IndexedDB
  • Cloud Sync: When logged in, data is synced to our secure servers for backup and cross-device access
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords: Stored using bcrypt hashing algorithm

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your vocabulary data with other users
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Track your browsing history beyond word lookups

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download your vocabulary data in JSON format
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out: Use the extension offline without creating an account

7. Data Retention

  • Your vocabulary data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts and data are permanently removed within 30 days
  • Local storage data persists until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension

8. Children's Privacy

Voca Web is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Open Source

Voca Web is open source software. You can review our code and security practices on GitHub.
